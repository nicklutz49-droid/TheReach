# TheReach

Worldbuilding for **The Reach** — a setting bible for a limited interstellar war between the Holy Terran Imperium and the Union of Free Systems, fought in the contested frontier both sides call the Reach.

Start with [The Reach — Worldbuilding Document](The%20Reach%20—%20Worldbuilding%20Document.md), the hub: the big picture, a quick-reference canon, and an index into the deep lore in [`lore/`](lore/):

1. **History** — the Concord of Man, the Sundering, the road to war
2. **The Imperium** — the great houses, the emperor, the Guilds
3. **The Union** — the Directory, the franchise, the braid
4. **The war: the ground** — doctrine, Halvard's World, the Bestiary
5. **The war: the void** — fleets, the press, the Emperor's Wager
6. **Clients and the fringe** — the small powers, the aliens, the machines
7. **The siege-breakers** — the mechs, their walkers, their legends, their cults
